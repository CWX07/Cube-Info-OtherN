    Other Notation

    Middle Layer Notation

    In the image shown below, the white is facing up while red is facing front

    M

    Turn the middle layer between L and R up

    M'

    Turn the middle layer between L and R down

    S

    Turn the middle layer between F and B right

    S'

    Turn the middle layer between F and B left

    E

    Turn the middle layer between U and D right

    E'

    Turn the middle layer between U and D left

    Cube Rotation Notation

    In the image shown below, the white is facing up while red is facing front

    x

    Rotate the cube to the top

    x'

    Rotate the cube to the bottom

    y

    Rotate the cube to your left

    y'

    Rotate the cube to your right

    z

    Rotate the cube 90 degree to make the left side of the cube facing up. Then do a x move

    z'

    Rotate the cube -90 degree to make right side of the cube facing up and do a x move
